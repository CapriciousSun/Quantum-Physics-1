## Eigenvalue Expansion
In terms of [[Dirac Notation|Dirac notation]], the expansion of a wavefunction $ψ = c_{1}ψ_{1} + c_{2}ψ_{2}$ is written as the following 
#### $$\begin{gather}
c_{1} = \langle ψ | ψ_{1} \rangle \\ \\
c_{2} = \langle ψ | ψ_{2} \rangle
\end{gather}$$
